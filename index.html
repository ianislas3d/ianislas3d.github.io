About Me

I am a 3D artist from Mexico with over a year and a half of experience in digital art and design.

My journey began in 2023, when I started learning the fundamentals of 3D art. In 2024, I shifted my focus toward non-photorealistic rendering (NPR), specializing in replicating 2D characters in 3D. This phase helped me build a strong foundation in Eevee rendering, including color math, floats and vectors, modeling techniques, and many transferable skills.

By mid-2024, I became increasingly interested in photorealistic rendering. I began studying Cycles shading and focused on creating small scenes using lighting setups, geometry nodes, and modeling for product visualization.

Now in 2025, I’ve refined my workflow, strengthened my existing skills, and continue to expand my knowledge—supported by the latest Blender updates.
